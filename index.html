WIP: High-Score Leaderboard from CSV file

This work-in-progress project makes use of file-reading and exception catching to extract data from a CSV of player usernames and their highscores.
The data is placed from a HashMap into binary search tree to rank the players in increasing score order. The breadth-first-search algorithm is used on a graph to find the difference in ranking between two players.
